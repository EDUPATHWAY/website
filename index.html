Ayush Raj

Goal: To become a successful teacher and inspire others.

Background: Ayush grew up in a small village where access to quality education was limited. Coming from a very poor family, he faced numerous challenges, including financial constraints and a lack of resources. Despite these hardships, his determination to learn and excel never waned.

Story: As a child, Ayush often walked miles to attend the nearest school, inspired by teachers who dedicated their time and energy to help students succeed. He vividly remembers a day when his teacher spent hours after class to help him understand a complex math problem. That moment ignited his passion for education.

Despite working odd jobs to support his family, Ayush remains hopeful, believing that education is key to breaking the cycle of poverty. With your support, he hopes to complete his degree and inspire the next generation.
